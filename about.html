About VIDEA

Our Values

Our organisation Values provide a framework for us to work towards creating a world where all young people and women in Uganda have what it takes to contribute to sustainable development of their communities.

Environmental Stewardship

The preservation of natural resources, conservation of biodiversity, & promotion of sustainable lifestyles

Community & Youth Empowerment

Supporting young people and women to build on local strengths in order to transform communities.

Collaboration & Partnership

We endeavour to build strong partnerships with individuals, organizations, and communities

Inclusivity & Diversity

We embrace diversity as a source of innovation and believe in promoting equal opportunities for all

Innovation & Technology

We embrace the potential of innovation and technology in finding sustainable solutions.

Ethical & Responsible action

We operate with a strong ethical framework and promotes responsible behaviour towards the environment.

Our Mission

To engage, educate, and empower young people and women in Uganda to become active agents of change for a safe, just, and equitable society

Our Vision

To create a world where all young people and women in Uganda have the skills, resources, and opportunities they need to achieve their ambitions, overcome obstacles, and contribute to the sustainable development of their communities.

VIDEA

  • Vijana for Sustainable Development and Environmental Action (VIDEA) is a non-profit making organization, accredited by the Uganda NGO Board in October 2021. We engage, educate, and empower young people and women in Uganda to become active agents of change for a safe, just, and equitable society.
